### What this data is about

This dataset provides comprehensive information on optical care reimbursements in France from 2009 to 2021, covering all health insurance schemes. The data, part of the monthly Open Damir series, includes monthly expenditures and reimbursement details, allowing for a detailed analysis of optical health insurance spending across various demographics and regions.

### How this data could be used

- Analyzing trends in optical care expenditures over time
- Evaluating the effectiveness and coverage of health insurance plans
- Conducting demographic studies on optical care needs and reimbursements
- Building predictive models for future healthcare costs and insurance claims
