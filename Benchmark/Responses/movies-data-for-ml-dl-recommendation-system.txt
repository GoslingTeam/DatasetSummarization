### What this data is about:
The dataset includes information on 5,000 movies released between 1987 and 2015. It comprises two files: one detailing movie credits with cast and crew information, and another providing movie attributes such as budget, genres, release date, revenue, and popularity. 

### How this data could be used:
- Developing movie recommendation systems using machine learning and deep learning models
- Analyzing trends and patterns in movie production and performance
- Creating visualizations of movie attributes and industry insights
