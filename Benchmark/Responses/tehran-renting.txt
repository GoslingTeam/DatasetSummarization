### What this data is about

The dataset provides information on rental properties in Tehran, including details on rent, security deposits, and various property features such as floor, area, and age. It includes data collected from multiple rental advertisement platforms, detailing the rental prices, deposit amounts, and attributes of different apartments and houses in various districts and regions of Tehran.

### How this data could be used

- Analyzing rental market trends and price variations across different neighborhoods in Tehran
- Evaluating the impact of property features on rental prices and security deposits
- Conducting exploratory data analysis for real estate investment opportunities
- Building predictive models for rental prices based on property characteristics
- Assessing the effect of inflation on rental and deposit values over time
