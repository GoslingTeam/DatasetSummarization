### What this data is about

The "Regression Technique EDA" dataset contains comprehensive data related to house prices, including various features of the properties such as type, zoning classification, lot dimensions, building characteristics, and sale conditions. This dataset is intended for performing exploratory data analysis (EDA) and applying regression techniques to predict house prices based on these features.

### How this data could be used

- Developing and training regression models to predict house prices.
- Performing exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover trends and patterns in real estate data.
- Evaluating the impact of different property features on house prices.
- Creating visualizations to better understand the relationships between property attributes and their sale prices.
- Conducting comparative analysis between different types of residential properties.
