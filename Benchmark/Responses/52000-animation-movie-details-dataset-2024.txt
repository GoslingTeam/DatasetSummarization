### What this data is about

This dataset, titled "52,000 Animation Movie Details (2024)," contains detailed information about 52,000 animation movies. The data was scraped from the TMDB API and includes various attributes such as titles, vote averages, release dates, revenues, runtimes, and more, providing a comprehensive overview of animation movies.

### How this data could be used

- Analyzing the popularity of animation movies across different genres
- Training machine learning models to predict movie ratings
- Extracting insights on budget and revenue trends in the animation industry
- Exploring the relationship between runtime and movie popularity
- Identifying common languages and production companies in animation movies
- Creating recommendation systems for animation movie enthusiasts
- Analyzing release date patterns and their impact on revenue
