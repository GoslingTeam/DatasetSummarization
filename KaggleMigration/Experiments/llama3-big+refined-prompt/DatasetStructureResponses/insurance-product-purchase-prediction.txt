ЗАГОЛОВОК ДАТАСЕТА: Insurance Product Purchase Prediction
ПОДЗАГОЛОВОК ДАТАСЕТА: Prediction based on shopping history

ОПИСАНИЕ ДАТАСЕТА: This dataset is used for predicting whether a customer will purchase an insurance product based on their shopping history and other demographic information.

СОДЕРЖИМО ФАЙЛА ДАТАСЕТА:
The file contains data about customers, including their ID, shopping point, record type, day, time, state, location, group size, homeowner status, car age and value, risk factor, age of oldest and youngest family members, marital status, previous purchase history, and cost.
